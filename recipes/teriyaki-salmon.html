Teriyaki Salmon

Description

Teriyaki glazed salmon in a matter of minutes! Pair it with some white rice or even noodles and you have a meal all ready to go.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients
  • Salmon Fillets Cut into 2 fillets each
Glaze Ingredients
    cid
  • 5 tbsp Kikkoman Teriyaki Sauce
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 200C.
  2. Rinse and pat dry salmon. Place in oven proof dish lined with foil. With a sharp knife, score the skin (optional).
  3. Pour sauce mix onto salmon pieces, lifting up salmon fillets so sauce can run below.
  4. Pop into oven for 10 minutes (or increase to 12 mins, if you prefer). Garnish with sesame seeds. Scoop out sauce onto rice. Done
